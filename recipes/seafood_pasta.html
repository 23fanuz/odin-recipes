Seafood Pasta

Description

This seafood pasta recipe combines buttery scallops and tender shrimp with a garlicky white wine sauce, served over linguine. Cherry tomatoes, shallots, and parsley add vibrant flavor, while the sauce remains light and bright, letting the seafood shine.

It’s easy to customize, whether swapping scallops for more shrimp or adding a splash of cream for richness. Ready in under 40 minutes, it’s an elegant yet simple dish perfect for weeknight dinners.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of linguine pasta
  • 1 shallot
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1.5 pounds of cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 bunch of parsley
  • 10-12 large scallops
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • A drizzle of olive oil
  • 1 cup of white wine
  • 10 ounces of peeled and deveined shrimp
  • 3 tablespoons of butter

Steps

  1. Boil salted water and cook linguine.
  2. Chop shallots, garlic, tomatoes, and parsley.
  3. Drain pasta and save some water.
  4. Pat scallops dry, season, and sear them until golden, then set aside.
  5. Sauté shallots, add tomatoes, garlic, and cook.
  6. Add wine, seasonings, and simmer.
  7. Add shrimp and cook until pink.
  8. Combine pasta, parsley, butter, and reserved water.
  9. Toss, season to taste, and serve with scallops on top.
