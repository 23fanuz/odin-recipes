Simple Tuna Salad Sandwich

Description

This simple tuna salad sandwich is a quick, satisfying meal that’s packed with flavor. Tender tuna is mixed with creamy mayo and a hint of mustard for a tangy kick, with the option to add crunchy celery or onion for extra texture.

Seasoned to taste with salt and pepper, the tuna salad is layered between two slices of bread, topped with fresh lettuce or juicy tomatoes. Perfect for a light lunch or an easy dinner, it’s both nutritious and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 can of tuna (drained)
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon mustard (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped celery or onion (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 slices of bread (toasted if preferred)
  • Lettuce or tomato for topping (optional)

Steps

  1. Drain the can of tuna and place it in a bowl.
  2. Add 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise and 1 teaspoon of mustard (if using).
  3. Mix in 1 tablespoon of chopped celery or onion (optional).
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste, then mix until well combined.
  5. Spread the tuna mixture evenly on one slice of bread.
  6. Top with lettuce or tomato (optional), then add the second slice of bread.
  7. Slice and serve!
