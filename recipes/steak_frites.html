Steak Frites

Description

Steak frites is a simple yet mouthwatering meal featuring a perfectly seared, juicy steak paired with crispy golden fries.

The tender steak, cooked in butter and seasoned to perfection, balances beautifully with the crispy fries, making it a satisfying, restaurant-quality dish. It’s easy to prepare and full of bold flavors, perfect for a cozy night in or an elevated dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 steaks (ribeye or sirloin)
  • 4 large potatoes (for fries)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Fresh herbs (optional, like thyme or parsley)

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes into fries, toss with olive oil, salt, and pepper, and bake at 425°F (220°C) for 30-35 minutes until crispy.
  2. Season steaks with salt and pepper, and sear in butter over medium-high heat, 3-4 minutes per side for medium-rare.
  3. Serve steak with fries on the side, garnish with fresh herbs if desired.
